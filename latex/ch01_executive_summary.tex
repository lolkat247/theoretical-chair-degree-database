\section{Executive Summary}

TCD Stocking Inc.\ is a restaurant food-item wholesaler based in California that sources a large portion of its
product from East Asia and Latin America. The company operates three warehouse locations and supplies a wide range
of food products including produce, meat, dairy, bakery, deli, frozen, and shelf-stable goods. Currently, the
company lacks a centralized system for tracking inventory levels across warehouses, managing customer information,
recording purchase history, and calculating costs associated with importing and shipping. This proposal presents a
relational database solution built on MariaDB designed to consolidate inventory management, customer records, vendor
information, and invoice processing into a single normalized system, enabling TCD Stocking Inc.\ to maintain
accurate stock levels, fulfill orders efficiently, and support sound financial decision-making.
